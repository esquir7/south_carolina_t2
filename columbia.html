Welcome to Columbia

Columbia is the capital city of South Carolina.

It is known for its history, cultural amenities, and being home to the University of South Carolina.

Things to Do in Columbia

  1. Visit the South Carolina State House
  2. Explore Riverbanks Zoo & Garden
  3. Walk through the Columbia Museum of Art
  4. Stroll along the Congaree Vista
  5. Attend a game at Williams-Brice Stadium
